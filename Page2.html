MY EXPERIENCE

IFIX IOS STUDIO

Technician

I got into tech stuff when i'm Form 1. I kept taking apart and fixing my own phone, and I got pretty good at it. By Form 3, "Abang Man" from iFix iOS Studio noticed my skills and offered me a part-time job at his place in Setia Alam. Working there as a technician, I dived into the world of fixing phones. I've fixed over 100 phones, mostly iPhones. This tech journey started when I was young, and it's not just a personal hobby; it shows how dedicated I am to this tech stuff.

HERMANOS BARBERSHOP

Barber

Also since the pandemic, I got into cutting hair. I learned by watching others and YouTube. My first try was on my sister, and it took me all day from 8 am to 8 pm, but it turned out okay. To get faster and better, I practiced on family and friends, then started helping people outside my circle. In 2021, I began offering home service, cutting hair at people's houses. I shared my work on social media, and that's how Hermanos Barbershop found me on Instagram. They asked me to join them, and they've been guiding me ever since. I still work there today, enjoying every moment of this unexpected journey into the world of hair cutting..
